How I made this What is this?

The U.S. military is a major polluter. Since the beginning of the Global War on Terror in 2001, the military has produced more than 1.2 billion metric tons of greenhouse gases. Khert, 22

The pentagon is locked into massive contributions to climate change for years to come. All because of its dependence on existing aircraft and warships. Lewis, 22

President Obama, in 2009 signed executive order 13514. Which forced all federal agencies to "increase energy efficiency" and "measure, report, an reduce their greenhouse gas emissions".

Created in the wake of climate change policies, the Great Green Fleet used an alternative fuel to that of fossil fuels. Presented as another method to fuel the United States Navy.

The Great Green Fleet comprised of its alternative fuel, was actually 90% petroleum and 10% animal fat. Essentially reducing the emmisions by ~10%. Certainly a strive in the right direction for sure, but perhaps overhyped.

The United States military, its hefty contributions and all, will be drastically effected by climate change. Governmental collapse, global trade - for food and other commodities - failing. Mass panic and flight will almost certainly occur.
